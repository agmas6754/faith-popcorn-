Faith Popcorn

The Futurist Who Decodes Tomorrow — and Advises Healthcare

1. Tracking

Faith Popcorn is not a doctor, nor a lab researcher. She is a futurist — a professional forecaster who helps industries prepare for what's next. For nearly five decades, her company BrainReserve has guided Fortune 500 companies through cultural shifts.

Healthcare organizations hire Popcorn to understand what patients will want years ahead. She predicted virtual health, mental wellness apps, and personalized medicine before they became standard.
